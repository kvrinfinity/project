Cookies Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit websites. They help improve your browsing experience by remembering preferences, login info, and tracking usage statistics.

2. How We Use Cookies

KVR Infinity uses cookies to:

  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Understand how visitors interact with our platform
  • Maintain login sessions
  • Track traffic and usage patterns using analytics tools

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the operation of our website
  • Analytical Cookies: Help us improve user experience by analyzing usage data
  • Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences and settings
  • Third-party Cookies: Set by third-party services like Google Analytics

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on our website.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (such as Google, Meta) that set their own cookies to collect statistics or serve personalized content. We do not control these cookies.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy occasionally. We recommend reviewing it periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Cookies Policy, please contact us at support@kvrinfinity.com.
