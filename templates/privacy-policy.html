Privacy & Policy

1. Introduction

At KVR Infinity, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you interact with our platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Info: Name, email address, contact number
  • Educational Data: Progress, performance, attendance
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device info
  • Usage Data: Access logs, time spent, preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and manage educational services
  • To personalize user experiences
  • To send updates, reports, and notifications
  • To improve platform performance and security

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared only with trusted partners (e.g., payment gateways, analytics services) as necessary to provide our services.

5. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your data. All team members sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA).

6. Your Rights

  • Right to access, update, or delete your data
  • Right to request data portability
  • Right to opt out of non-essential communication

7. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance user experience and collect analytics. See our Cookies Policy for more details.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from users under 13 years of age. If you're a parent and suspect your child has shared data, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of our platform indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact Us

For any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy, please contact us at privacy@kvrinfinity.com.
